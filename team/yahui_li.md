### Overview
Yahui Li (李雅慧) is an MSc student in MoS Lab, Department of Civil Engineering, Tsinghua University. Her interests include intelligent transportation systems, rail transit operations, emergency response decision support, and data-driven transportation policy analysis.

### Education

- Exchange Student in Industrial and Systems Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, *Sep 2025 - Dec 2025*
- B.Eng. in Intelligent Transportation Engineering, Beijing Jiaotong University, *Sep 2022 - Jun 2026*

### Research Experience
- Agent-based intelligent assistant for rail transit emergency decision-making, *May 2023 - May 2024*
  - Built a professional knowledge base for railway emergency response using emergency cases, response plans, and industry technical regulations.
  - Supported rapid retrieval by splitting long documents into knowledge chunks and storing them in a vector database.
  - Generated structured emergency response strategies by matching user scenarios with relevant knowledge and large language models.
- Autonomous identification and handling system for airborne light floating objects along railway perimeters, *May 2024 - May 2025*
  - Designed the system architecture integrating object detection, depth-camera localization, UAV obstacle avoidance, and robotic-arm handling.
  - Modeled the robotic arm with Denavit-Hartenberg parameters and validated workspace constraints through MATLAB simulation.
